Beginner Plan

Perfect for those new to fitness or returning after a long break. This plan focuses on building fundamental strength and endurance with manageable workouts.

Weekly Schedule

Monday/Thursday

Chest Workout
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Chest Fly: 2 sets of 12 reps

Tuesday/Friday

Back Workout
  • Assisted Pull-ups: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Seated Row: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown: 2 sets of 12 reps

Wednesday/Saturday

Legs Workout
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges: 2 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12 reps

Daily

Cardio
  • 10 min light cardio (walking/jogging)

Sunday

Rest Day
  • Active recovery recommended
  • Stretching or light yoga

Nutrition Guidance

Protein Intake

Aim for 1.6-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight daily to support muscle growth.

Carbs Are Fuel

Don't fear carbohydrates - they provide energy for your workouts.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 3 liters of water daily, more on workout days.
